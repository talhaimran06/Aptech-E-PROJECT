Govt approves increase in prices of 146 essential life-saving medicines

The caretaker federal cabinet on Thursday approved an increase in the prices of 146 essential life-saving
medicines, state-run Radio Pakistan Reported.

The cabinet meeting was chaired by interim Prime Minister AnwaarulHaqKakar in Islamabad. According to the report, the surge in prices was
recommended by the Ministry of National Health considering the rising prices of raw materials in the global market


During the meeting, PM Kakar said the government was taking “every possible step” for the provision of medicines to the common man at a suitable price. He added that the government was devising policies that would not only benefit the common man but also the pharmaceutical industry.
